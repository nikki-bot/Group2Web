All about FreshEats

FreshEats began as Countdown's answer to simple shopping and meal preparation. We introduced meal kits. One simple recipe card paired with all of the required ingredients chopped and prepped ready to be made by you.

Simply find the meal recipe card you are after. We will assemble a meal kit for you and add it to your regular household needs.

We offer plans which are as flexible as your needs. If you want a simple dinner for one, you can order one meal kit as Eats for One. If you are a group of flatmates we send enough to feed a crowd. Our Eats for a Flat. This could meet your need for a one off dinner party.

If you want to cook for a friend or special someone we offer Eats for Two which is a meal kit with enough foor for two people. If you want one, two, three, four or even five meal kits per week to meet all of your dinner needs then sign up for a weekly plan!

Whatever you need we can provide it.

Innocent Packaging

Uniting with local Kiwi owned and operated packaging company Innocent Packaging ensures zero waste. Meal kits are packed with either compostable or recyclable products.

Countdown wants to make a positive difference by being eco conscious and offering meal kits to lessen food waste as well as packaging waste.






Meal kits can be collected with your online Click and Collect shopping. Try out one meal kit today, see how you go and sign up for a weekly plan.

We also deliver meal kits with online shopping delivery orders.

Otherwise find meal kits at our Delicatessen when you shop in store
