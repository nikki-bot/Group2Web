All about FreshEats

We started in 2020. The idea was simple. We wanted to combine weekly shopping with meal kits. All our customers could save time on shopping, meal preparation and having to choose what to cook for dinner. The solution? Meal kits. Simply packaged ingredients with a recipe card to enable our customer to cook dinner with their requirements in mind. Whether our customer is solving their problem of cooking for one or a crowd.. Whether they had a one off diner party or dessert to come up with. We had the answers for them with our simple to use plans.

Uniting with local Kiwi owned and operated packaging company Innocent Packaging ensures zero waste. Meal kits are packed with either compostable or recyclable products.

Simply choose the recipe you like from our Recipes page and add the ingreidents to your cart. We will asemble a meal kit for you and have it delivered to your door or simply Click and Collect it with all your regular household shop from your local Countdown store.
